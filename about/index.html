Writing software excites me. It engages my mind fully and energizes me in a self-perpetuating way that I have not experienced outside of programming. I love learning, and I love that learning is an inherent and lifelong component of being a good programmer. I love not only the process of writing software, but the end result as well. I love that people can interact with my creations and derive utility or amusement from them.

Ever since sharing my first text adventure game with friends and family, and seeing them play and explore in an environment I had fabricated, I have been inspired to continue learning and building to further share my creations with others.
